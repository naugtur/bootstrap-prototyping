Commercial use of this material includes using it in paid courses.
Therefore to use this material legaly there are three rules:
1. One cannot be paid money for teaching the course
2. Participants of the course are not supposed to pay for course if this material is to be used
3. Course is not a free addition to a paid service or product

Exceptions to this rule are only possible with individual written premition from the author.

So the material can be used to teach yourself or in free courses and meetings of developer/designer communities without any limitations.
